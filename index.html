What is PhoneGuard?

PhoneGuard is a Python-based CCTV system that uses your phone's camera (via IP Webcam app) to detect motion in a specific area and instantly alerts you on Telegram with a photo snapshot.

📱

Phone as Camera

Use any Android phone as a surveillance camera over Wi-Fi using the IP Webcam app.

🎯

Smart Zone Detection

Draw a box on screen to select exactly which area to monitor. Only alerts on motion inside that zone.

🤖

Telegram Bot Alerts

Sends you a photo the moment motion is detected. Use /check command to verify everything is working.
